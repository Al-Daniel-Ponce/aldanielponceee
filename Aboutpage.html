About The,

The Tangub City Global College had its annual celebration of Intramurals - Lux Mundis Cup with the theme "MAJESTIC FUSION". The Event had a Five-Day Majestic Fusions of Academic Events, sports events, and special events. This event aim to enable education learning and the sharing of knowledge among Lux Mundis and provide important tool for helping the students develop their critical-thinking skills. Also, Most importantly, To develop the value of camaraderie and build up relations among students of the 6 institute of Tcgc.
